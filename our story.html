Our Journey

The conviction in our mission

This cafe website began as part of a course project a creative challenge that turned into something we truly enjoyed building. While it started from a requirement, it quickly became more than that: a chance to design a space that reflects warmth, good vibes, and a love for coffee culture.

Our Story☕

We imagined a café that serves rich Arabica beans sourced from Colombia and Ethiopia, known for their smooth, bold flavor. Whether real or imagined, this site is a celebration of what a cozy café could be welcoming, peaceful, and brewed with care.

At this website was originally created as a course assignment, but it reflects our love for coffee and good design. We imagined a cafe that uses only the best Arabica beans, with a calm and welcoming vibe for everyone who walks in. It’s a small project with a big heart just like the perfect cup of coffee.
